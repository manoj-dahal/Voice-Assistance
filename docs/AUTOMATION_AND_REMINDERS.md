# Automation and Reminders

## Automations

The Macros view contains workflow templates for:

- daily briefing;
- focus sprint;
- meeting preparation;
- evening review.

Before a workflow runs, ERA presents its purpose, steps, impact, and risk.

## Timers

Example:

```text
Start a 25 minute timer.
```

Timers are local to the active browser session and produce visual, spoken, and approved notification feedback.

## Durable reminders

Examples:

```text
Remind me in 30 minutes to review the project.
Remind me every day to check the task board.
Remind me every week to back up ERA.
```

Reminders persist in browser local storage and recover after reload.

## Reminder dock

Users can:

- create a reminder manually;
- choose a relative minute delay;
- inspect due time;
- identify recurring reminders;
- delete reminders.

## Safety boundary

Current automations do not silently:

- send communication;
- purchase products;
- change accounts;
- edit external calendars;
- delete external data;
- execute terminal commands.

Future external actions must use scoped connectors and confirmation checkpoints.
